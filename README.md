# ChoreFlow 🧼

> **The ultimate, local-first helper to prove to your housemates that you DO, in fact, wash the dishes.**

Are your roommates constantly complaining that the sink is overflowing? Do they claim you *"never do your share"* while you feel like you are practically living in the kitchen, scrubbing pans every single day? 

No more endless arguments, chore standoffs, or passive-aggressive sticky notes on the fridge. It's time to bring **hard, empirical data** to the dinner table. 

**ChoreFlow** is a beautiful, modern, and private web tracker designed specifically to record your home meals and cleanup duties. The next time someone says you don't clean enough, simply open your dashboard and present the facts.

---

## ✨ Features designed for "Debate Settlement"

* **📊 Proof-Centered Metrics**:
  * **Meals at Home**: Displays the total count and percentage of meals you ate at home out of all logged times.
  * **Washing Dishes**: Displays the exact percentage of times **you** cleaned up after eating at home. (Perfect for showing: *"I ate here 4 times, and I washed dishes 4 times. That is a 100% record."*)
* **📅 Weekly Overview Grid**: A clean, visual list showing the last 7 calendar days with badges indicating whether you ate at home (`Home` 🏠) or out (`Out` 🍕), and whether you washed (`Washed` 🧼).
* **🕒 Smart Time-Gated Wizard**: To prevent tedious manual data entry, the app prompts you dynamically based on the current hour:
  * **Before 12:00 PM**: No questions—just view your clean dashboard.
  * **12:00 PM to 8:00 PM**: Prompts only for **Lunch** questions.
  * **After 8:00 PM**: Prompts for **Dinner** questions as well (or dinner only if lunch was logged earlier).
* **⏩ Prominent Skip Button**: In a rush? Bypass questions with one tap to check the dashboard instantly.

---

## 🔒 100% Local & Private (Zero Database, Zero Cloud)

We believe that your eating habits and chore records are your business alone. 

* **No server backend, no third-party APIs, and no tracking.**
* **All data is saved entirely in your browser's `localStorage`.**
* **Zero setup**: The app works out of the box, even offline. Just open it and start logging.
* **Multi-user ready**: Different housemates can create separate usernames locally on the same device to keep individual records.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start in 15 seconds

1. Download this repository.
2. Double-click **`index.html`** to open the tracker immediately in any modern web browser (no installation, compilers, or server execution required!).
3. Switch to the **Register** tab, type a local username (e.g. `Stefano`), and set a password.
4. Log in, and the time-gated questionnaire will guide you through your lunch or dinner statuses.
5. If someone accuses you of neglecting duties, scroll to the bottom to find **System Settings**, type `RESET` to start a fresh tracking season if needed, or simply let the charts do the talking.
